Description

"Minions: The Rise of Gru" is a comedic animated prequel set in the 1970s. 12-year-old Gru aspires to join the Vicious 6 supervillain group, aided by his loyal Minions. This origin story follows Gru's misadventures as he learns about friendship and pursuing dreams, all while causing mischief with his yellow companions.
